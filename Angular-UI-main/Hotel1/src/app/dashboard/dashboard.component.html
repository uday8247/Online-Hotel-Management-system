Single Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of Single Rooms are generally between 37 m² to 45 m².

Double Room

A double room contains is intended for two people, the bed will either be a Double, Queen or King size bed. This room is for couples.There will be only one bed.

Triple Room

A room suitable for up to 3 guests. The room contains two regular beds and one extra bed in a superior twin room.

Twin Room

The twin room and double room seem to be the same. Both types of these hotel rooms are designed for two people. The twin room has two single beds. The double room has one double bed, so-called, a marital bed.

Quad Room

Quad rooms sleep 4 in one big room.Connecting rooms with 2 beds is also available

king Room
